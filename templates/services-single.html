Asset Integrity

ENCENDER has over the decade provided asset integrity solutions:


Pipline Integrity Program

Our pipeline integrity program, is robust to handle pipe scans down to 50feet beneath the earth surface.
We conduct these advanced NDT inspections on pipelines and they are as follows:

  • Long Range Ultrasonic Testing
  • Time of Flight Diffraction on pipeline welds
  • Coating Defect Survey
  • Basic NDT Inspections(UTM,DPI,MPI,PT)
  • Cathodic Protection Installation and maintenance

Vessel Integrity Program

With our vessel integrity program, we carry out our inspections based on the internationally approved and recognized standard which could vary because it is tailored to the needs of our clients.
We carry out basic NDT inspections and advanced NDT inspections on pressure vessels and they are as follows:

  • Corrosion Mapping of Pressure Vessels
  • Time of Flight Diffraction on Vessel welds
  • Phrase Array Ultrasonic Test
  • Basic NDT Inspections(UTM,DPI,MPI,PT)

We provide technical and professional consultancy in provision of these solutions depending on the given scope of activities. Working with Encender would give clients the necessary excellent posture that is primary to deliver the project.
